For patients

Everything you need before your visit

First-visit info, financial options, patient forms, and CDCP details — all in one place.

  • Your first visit

    We'll start with a comprehensive exam, necessary X-rays, and a chat about your dental history and goals. You'll leave with a written treatment plan and no surprises. Plan about 60 minutes for your first appointment.

    Book your first visit

  • Financial options

    We direct-bill most major insurance providers and offer flexible payment plans for larger treatments. Ask us about split-payment options for orthodontics, implants, and full-mouth restorations.

    Ask about payment plans

  • Patient forms

    Save time at your first visit by filling out our new-patient paperwork in advance. Forms are sent by email after booking.

    Book to receive forms

  • Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)

    We're a participating CDCP provider. If you qualify for CDCP coverage, bring your plan details to your first visit — we'll handle the rest.
