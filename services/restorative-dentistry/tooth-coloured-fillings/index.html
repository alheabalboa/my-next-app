Restorative Dentistry

Tooth-Coloured Fillings

Composite and ceramic fillings that restore a decayed or broken tooth without the look of metal.

Book AppointmentCall 403-300-2233

What to expect

A conservative option for small to medium cavities. We bond, shape, and polish the material so the restoration disappears into the surrounding enamel.

Benefits of tooth-coloured fillings

  • Aesthetic & natural look — the filling material matches your tooth shade, making the restoration virtually invisible when you smile or speak.
  • Preserves healthy tooth structure — composite fillings bond directly to the tooth, allowing more conservative preparations and less drilling.
  • Mercury-free alternative — a modern, natural material option without metal components.
  • Durable & long-lasting — with proper care, composite fillings last many years and withstand normal chewing forces.

When to book

  • A cavity has been diagnosed and is small enough for a filling
  • An older silver filling is cracked or stained
  • You want a more aesthetic repair of a chipped front tooth
  • You have a mild allergy or sensitivity to amalgam

Frequently asked questions

Are tooth-coloured fillings better than silver ones?

Tooth-coloured fillings offer superior aesthetics and bond directly to teeth, making them preferred for visible areas thanks to their natural appearance.

How long do they last?

With proper oral hygiene, composite fillings typically last between 5 and 10 years or longer, with regular dental checkups helping ensure durability.

Can old metal fillings be replaced with tooth-coloured ones?

Yes — many patients opt to replace older metal fillings with composite restorations for cosmetic improvement, though your dentist will evaluate suitability.

Is the procedure painful?

Most patients experience minimal or no discomfort, as local anesthesia is typically administered and the procedure is minimally invasive.
