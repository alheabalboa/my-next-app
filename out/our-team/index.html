Meet our team

The people behind your smile

Warm, experienced, and never in a hurry — our dentists treat every patient the way we'd want our own family treated.

  • Dentist

    Dr. Kevin R. Kalin

    DDS, University of Alberta School of Dentistry. Completed an oral and maxillofacial surgery residency at Harvard / Massachusetts General Hospital, with focused experience in wisdom-tooth extraction, bone grafting, and implant placement. Outside of the clinic, enjoys hockey, tennis, basketball, biking, and hiking.

  • General Dentist

    Dr. Mivea Matta

    Began practising dentistry in 2012 after graduating in Hyderabad, India. Brings a decade of private and public experience to Calgary, with a focus on patient-centred care. Enjoys hiking, swimming, camping, and painting.

  • Dentist

    Dr. Sandeep Dhaliwal

    BDS, Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (Punjab). Certified by the National Dental Examination Board of Canada and registered with the Alberta Dental Association. Known for a warm, patient chair-side manner and an emphasis on continuing education. Family-oriented; enjoys reading and travel.

  • Dentist

    Dr. Ebraheem Alhammadi

    DDS, Dalhousie University. Approaches dentistry as a blend of science, precision, and craft, with a calm, reassuring manner that puts anxious patients at ease. Interests include fitness, travel, and food.
