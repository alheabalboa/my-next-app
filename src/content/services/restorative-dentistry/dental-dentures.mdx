## What to expect

After impressions and a bite check, we fit a custom denture calibrated to your jaw. Adjustments over the following weeks fine-tune comfort and fit.

## Benefits of dental dentures

- **Restore confidence in your smile** — dentures fill the gaps left by missing teeth, helping you smile without hesitation.
- **Improve chewing and speaking** — well-fitting dentures let you enjoy more of your favourite foods and speak clearly without discomfort.
- **Support facial muscles** — dentures provide structure to your cheeks and lips, helping maintain a more youthful appearance.
- **Custom fit for everyday comfort** — each set is carefully tailored to your mouth for a snug, reliable fit and all-day wearability.

## When to book

- You're missing several teeth and want a removable option
- Your current denture is loose, sore, or worn
- You want to explore implant-supported dentures for more stability
- You need an immediate denture after extractions

## Frequently asked questions

<details open>
<summary>What types of dentures are available?</summary>

Full dentures (for complete tooth loss), partial dentures (for multiple missing teeth), and immediate dentures (placed the same day teeth are removed).

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long do dentures last?</summary>

With proper care, dentures typically last 5 to 10 years. They may need occasional adjustments or replacement due to natural changes in your mouth.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Are dentures uncomfortable?</summary>

Today's dentures are designed for a comfortable fit. There's a short adjustment period, but most patients adapt quickly.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Do I have to take dentures out at night?</summary>

Yes — it's generally recommended to remove dentures at night to allow your gums to rest and to maintain oral hygiene.

</details>
