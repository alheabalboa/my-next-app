## What to expect

We plan the case with 3D imaging, place the implant, and let it integrate with the jawbone over a few months before attaching the final restoration. Free consults available.

## Benefits of dental implants

- **Permanent tooth replacement** — implants are securely anchored in the jawbone, offering unmatched stability and long-term results.
- **Restore natural look and feel** — implants look, feel, and function like natural teeth, making them virtually indistinguishable.
- **Preserve jawbone health** — unlike traditional dentures or bridges, implants stimulate bone growth and prevent bone loss.
- **No slipping or adhesives** — implants stay fixed in place, with no shifting, clicking, or need for removal.

## When to book

- You're missing one or more teeth
- A denture or bridge no longer feels stable
- You want the longest-lasting tooth replacement option
- You'd like to prevent bone loss where a tooth was lost
