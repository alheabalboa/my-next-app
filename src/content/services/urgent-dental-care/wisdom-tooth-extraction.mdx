## What to expect

We review a panoramic X-ray, discuss sedation options, and plan the extraction. Most patients are back to normal activities in a few days.

## Benefits of wisdom tooth extraction

- **Prevents crowding** — removes problematic teeth that can push others out of alignment.
- **Reduces infection risk** — eliminates partially erupted teeth that are difficult to clean and prone to infection.
- **Relieves pain** — eases discomfort caused by impacted or misaligned wisdom teeth.
- **Improves oral hygiene** — creates easier access for brushing and flossing, promoting better gum health.

## When to book

- Recurring pain or swelling at the back of the jaw
- A dentist has flagged impacted wisdom teeth
- Your orthodontist recommends removal before or after treatment
- Food keeps getting trapped near partially erupted molars
