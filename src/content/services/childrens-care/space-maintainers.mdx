## What to expect

Custom-fit at a short visit and monitored at regular checkups. Removed once the adult tooth is ready to erupt.

## Benefits of space maintainers

- **Prevents future orthodontic problems** — keeps teeth in proper positions, reducing the risk of crowding, misalignment, and the need for braces later.
- **Supports proper jaw and tooth development** — maintaining space allows permanent teeth to grow in where they belong.
- **Customized for comfort and fit** — tailored to your child's unique needs for maximum comfort and effectiveness.
- **Minimizes costly corrections later** — early intervention helps avoid more extensive orthodontic or surgical treatments down the road.

## When to book

- A baby tooth was lost to decay or injury well before its time
- Your dentist flagged an early extraction
- The neighbouring teeth are starting to tilt into the gap
- You want to avoid orthodontics later if possible

## Frequently asked questions

<details open>
<summary>When is a space maintainer needed?</summary>

Typically when a baby tooth is lost prematurely due to decay or injury, and the permanent tooth isn't yet ready to emerge.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long does it stay in place?</summary>

It remains in the mouth until the permanent tooth begins to erupt. We monitor progress and remove it at the appropriate time.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Will my child be able to eat and talk normally?</summary>

Yes — after a short adjustment period, most children adapt easily and continue regular daily activities with minimal disruption.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Is the space maintainer painful?</summary>

No. The device is custom-made for comfort and carefully fitted. Some mild initial pressure may occur but subsides quickly.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does it require special care?</summary>

Yes — regular brushing and flossing are important, and avoiding sticky or hard foods helps prevent damage. We provide guidance on proper maintenance.

</details>
