## What to expect

Regular adjustment visits every four to six weeks. Most comprehensive cases finish in 18 to 24 months, with retainers afterward.

## Benefits of traditional braces

- **Effective for complex cases** — capable of correcting severe misalignment and bite problems.
- **Durable and reliable** — strong materials provide consistent tooth movement over time.
- **Customizable options** — choose from metal or ceramic brackets to suit your preference.
- **Long-term results** — proven to create lasting improvements in dental alignment.

## When to book

- Complex crowding or rotation that aligners can't handle
- A significant bite correction is needed
- You prefer the certainty of a fixed appliance
- You want an affordable orthodontic option
