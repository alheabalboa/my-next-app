## What to expect

After 3D scans and a treatment plan, you'll wear each aligner for about a week. Most cases complete in six to eighteen months. Free consults available.

## Benefits of Invisalign

- **Nearly invisible** — clear aligners provide subtle, discreet treatment compared to traditional braces.
- **Removable convenience** — eat, brush, and floss normally by removing your aligners when needed.
- **Comfortable fit** — smooth plastic trays reduce irritation to cheeks and gums.
- **Predictable results** — custom treatment plans with digital imaging track progress effectively.

## When to book

- Crowded or crooked teeth bother you
- You declined braces as a teen and want another option
- Bite shifts have returned after previous orthodontics
- You want a discreet option for work or social reasons

<summary>How long does Invisalign treatment take?</summary>

Yes — they're designed to be removed for eating and cleaning.
