## What to expect

Performed with a dental laser or fine instruments under local numbing. Healing is typically quick, with visible results after a few days.

## Benefits of gum contouring

- **Improves smile aesthetics** — balances the gumline to reveal more natural tooth surface for a confident smile.
- **Enhances oral health** — reduces areas where plaque and bacteria accumulate under excessive gums.
- **Quick recovery** — minimally invasive procedure with a short healing time.
- **Boosts confidence** — a more symmetrical smile improves self-esteem and appearance.

## When to book

- Your smile looks 'gummy' when you grin
- Gumline is uneven across the front teeth
- You're planning veneers and want the frame to match
- A tooth looks shorter than its neighbour
