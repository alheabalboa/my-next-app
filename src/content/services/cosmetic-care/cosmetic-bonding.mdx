## What to expect

A more conservative option than veneers, preserving most of the natural tooth. Bonding is hand-sculpted, cured with light, and polished to match surrounding enamel.

## Benefits of cosmetic bonding

- **Quick and painless** — bonding is often completed in a single visit with minimal discomfort.
- **Cost-effective** — an affordable way to fix minor cosmetic issues without crowns or veneers.
- **Natural-looking results** — the tooth-coloured resin blends seamlessly with your natural teeth for a discreet enhancement.
- **Versatile treatment** — effective for repairing chips, closing small gaps, and covering stains or discolouration.

## When to book

- A small chip from biting or an accident
- A narrow gap between two front teeth
- A single tooth that looks slightly misshapen
- You want to try a cosmetic change before committing to veneers
