## What to expect

Choose a one-visit in-chair treatment or custom take-home trays you wear at night. We check sensitivity and existing dental work first so the result is even.

## Benefits of teeth whitening

- **Noticeable results, fast** — see visible improvements after just one treatment — perfect for special events or everyday confidence.
- **Boosted self-confidence** — a brighter smile enhances how attractive and self-assured you feel in social and professional settings.
- **Safe and monitored by a dentist** — professional treatments deliver safer, more even results than over-the-counter alternatives.
- **Customized to your smile** — treatment strength and duration are tailored to your tooth sensitivity and preferred shade.

## When to book

- Your teeth look duller or yellower than you'd like
- You have a wedding, graduation, or event coming up
- Drugstore strips aren't making a difference
- You want a controlled, professional approach to sensitivity

## Frequently asked questions

<details open>
<summary>Is professional teeth whitening safe?</summary>

Yes — professional whitening is safe when performed by a dentist, who uses proven methods to protect your enamel and gums.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long do whitening results last?</summary>

With good oral hygiene and regular touch-ups, results can last from several months to a year or more.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Will whitening make my teeth sensitive?</summary>

Some sensitivity is possible, but steps are taken to minimize it. Let your dentist know if you've had sensitivity issues in the past.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I whiten crowns or fillings?</summary>

Whitening only affects natural tooth enamel — existing restorations like crowns and fillings won't change colour.

</details>
