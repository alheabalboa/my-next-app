## What to expect

We remove the decayed portion, bond a composite resin matched to your tooth colour, and shape it to your bite. Most fillings are done in a single visit.

## Benefits of composite fillings

- **Natural appearance** — matches your tooth colour for a discreet, attractive restoration.
- **Durability** — strong enough to withstand daily chewing and biting forces.
- **Preserves tooth structure** — requires less removal of healthy tooth than metal fillings.
- **Versatile treatment** — suitable for repairing cavities, chips, and small fractures.

## When to book

- You've felt sudden sensitivity to cold or sweets
- You see a dark spot or pit in a tooth
- A dentist has flagged early decay on an X-ray
- An old metal filling is cracked, stained, or leaking
