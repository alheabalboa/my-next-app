## What are dental cleanings?

Dental cleanings are a vital preventive service performed by your dentist or hygienist to remove plaque, tartar, and stains from your teeth. The process targets areas regular brushing and flossing can miss, helping prevent tooth decay, gum disease, and bad breath while keeping your smile looking its best.

## The benefits of dental cleanings

- **Prevents gum disease** — removes harmful plaque and tartar that cause gum inflammation and infection.
- **Fights tooth decay** — cleans away buildup that leads to cavities, protecting your teeth long term.
- **Freshens breath** — eliminates odor-causing bacteria for a cleaner mouth and confident smile.
- **Enhances appearance** — polishing during cleanings brightens your teeth and restores natural shine.
