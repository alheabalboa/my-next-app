## What to expect

A comprehensive check of teeth, gums, bite, and soft tissue, paired with targeted digital X-rays when needed. We walk through every finding with you before anything is planned.

## Benefits of oral exams & X-rays

- **Early problem detection** — identify dental issues such as cavities, gum disease, or bone loss before symptoms appear.
- **Personalized treatment plans** — detailed exams let your dentist tailor care specifically to your needs.
- **Preventive care** — regular exams and X-rays help maintain healthy teeth and gums, preventing costly procedures later.
- **Peace of mind** — knowing your oral health status reduces anxiety and builds confidence in your smile.

## When to book

- You're overdue for a routine checkup (more than six months)
- You've moved and need a new dental home
- You're noticing discomfort, sensitivity, or a change in your bite
- You want baseline X-rays before cosmetic or orthodontic work

## Frequently asked questions

<details open>
<summary>How often should I get oral exams and X-rays?</summary>

Most patients benefit from exams and X-rays every six months, but your dentist may recommend a different schedule based on your oral health.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Are dental X-rays safe?</summary>

Yes — dental X-rays use very low radiation levels and are considered safe when performed regularly under professional care.

</details>

Exams can find cavities, gum disease, oral cancer, infections, and other dental problems before they worsen.
