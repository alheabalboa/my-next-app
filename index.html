Questions

Frequently asked

  • Are you accepting new patients?
    Yes — we welcome new patients of all ages, every day of the week.
  • Do you accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)?
    Yes. We're a CDCP-participating provider and can help you understand what's covered.
  • Do you handle dental emergencies?
    Yes. Call us at 403-300-2233 — we hold time every day for urgent cases like broken teeth, sudden pain, or lost crowns.
  • What are your hours?
    Mon–Sat 8 AM–8 PM · Sun 10 AM–5 PM — including evenings and Sundays.
  • Do you offer free consultations?
    Yes — consultations for dental implants, Invisalign, and wisdom-tooth removal are always complimentary.
  • Where are you located?
    6915 Rundlehorn Dr NE, Calgary, AB T1Y 3V4 — in NE Calgary.
  • Do you see children?
    Absolutely. We have a dedicated children's care program covering preventive care, sealants, and space maintainers.
  • Do you offer sedation for anxious patients?
    Yes. Ask us about our sedation dentistry options during your booking call — we'll find a comfort level that works for you.
